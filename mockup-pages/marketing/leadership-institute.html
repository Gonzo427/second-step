Are you coordinating a districtwide implementation of the Second Step program? If so, the Second Step Leadership Institute is an opportunity to learn directly from our experts about systemic implementation strategies and to collaborate with peers from around the country. From day one, the dynamic exchange of ideas generates valuable suggestions you can put to use in your schools. A full year of interactions with a professional learning community dedicated to social-emotional learning keeps you inspired and connected.

Second Step Leadership Program

Who is this for?

The institute is designed to support people who have the very specific role of coordinationg a distrct wide implementation of the Second Step program. You’ll join colleagues from around the country who also want to learn about districtwide implementation of the Second Step program.

Why choose this?

Participants will:

  • Learn to create and support a successful systemic implementation of the Second Step program
  • Develop, maintain, and benefit from a working professional learning community devoted to sustainable districtwide implementation of the Second Step program.
  • Create, implement, and refine a customized, comprehensive Second Step implementation plan for their district using the tools and templates provided.
