What products are available for Elementary?

Second Step Social Emotional Learning Program

Social-emotional learning curriculum and more for Early Learning–Grade 8.

The research-based Second Step program includes everything needed to make it easy for teachers to integrate social-emotional learning into their classrooms, which decreases problem behaviors and increases whole-school success by promoting self-regulation, safety, and support.

Learn More

Bullying Prevention Unit

Student lessons and staff training to create a positive school climate for Kindergarten–Grade 5.

Field-tested lessons are designed to make the best use of class time while videos, stories, and games model how to recognize, refuse, and report bullying. The Bullying Prevention Unit curriculum applies foundational social-emotional skills.

Learn More

Child Protection Unit

Keeping kids safe from abuse with staff training and student lessons for Early Learning–Grade 5.

Robust training helps develop child protection policies and procedures and shows how to recognize and respond to signs of abuse. The Child Protection Unit includes age-appropriate lessons teach students clear rules about touching safety. And family materials extend support to the home.

Learn More
