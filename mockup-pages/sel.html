Trusted By Schools

School leaders choose the Second Step program because it's trusted by 26,000+ schools worldwide. It aligns with district standards, and SEL improves student achievement.

Engaging And Useful

Students like the Second Step program because the lessons are engaging and the skills taught are useful in school, on the playground, and at home.

Easy To Use

Educators give the Second Step program a thumbs-up because it's easy to use and can improve classroom behavior, which improves learning.

Endorsed

The Second Step program has been endorsed by numerous organizations, including the Collaborative for Academic, Social, and Emotional Learning, for the quality of its research, training and teacher and family support. Learn more about how Second Step aligns with academic standards.
