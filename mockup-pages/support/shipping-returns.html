Outside the U.S. and Canada

Committee for Children uses UPS Worldwide Expedited for all shipments. Delivery times vary by country. Shipping charges are calculated in U.S. dollars based on the gross cost of the physical products you purchase using our online store.

Gross Product Cost Shipping Charge

In addition, you will be responsible for any additional destination-specific customs/duties/fees/taxes that may apply.

Country-Specific Item Exceptions

Before placing your order, make sure you are aware of any material limitations that customs officers place on imported goods in your country. For example, plush toys are considered "protected industry" in some countries. Retrieving any items from customs is your responsibility. In addition, Pencils (000099) and large/hallway posters (006110) cannot be shipped to addresses outside of the U.S., U.S. territories, Canada, or military addresses. Please contact our client support staff to discuss alternatives.

Our Gurantee and Returns

Committee for Children guarantees all materials.

Physical Items

If you are not completely satisfied, we accept returns or exchanges of items in resale condition up to ninety (90) days from the invoice date for a refund of the purchase price (not including shipping and handling).

Digital Subscriptions

We accept cancellations up to thirty (30) days from the invoice date for a refund of the purchase price (not including any applicable taxes and other charges). You will be responsible for all charges incurred with respect to fees processed before the cancellation of your subscription.

For further information on returns or exchanges, or to cancel a subscription, call our client support staff.

Alternate Carrier

If you wish to use a carrier other than UPS for your destination market, do not place your order in our online store.

Call our client support staff to request this.
